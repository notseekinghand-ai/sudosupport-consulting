The Path to Sovereignty

A proven, three-stage process for liberating your enterprise from proprietary ecosystems and establishing total control over your infrastructure.

1

Strategic Audit

Before the migration, we conduct a comprehensive analysis of your current infrastructure. We identify every proprietary dependency, calculate your "Microsoft Tax," and map out all data silos and telemetry exposure points. This phase provides a clear, data-driven picture of your current state and a precise roadmap for the evacuation.

2

Evacuation & Migration

This is the core execution phase. We perform a seamless, zero-downtime migration of your entire stack. This includes moving workstations to Fedora, core servers to RHEL, and productivity suites to our Private Cloud Stack (Nextcloud/LibreOffice). We handle all data transfer, application compatibility, and user training to ensure a smooth transition.

3

Continuous Sovereignty

Our engagement does not end at migration. We provide ongoing management and hardening of your new open-source fortress. This includes managing your private AI models, monitoring infrastructure health with sovereign tools, and ensuring your systems remain secure and independent. We ensure your metal stays yours, forever.
