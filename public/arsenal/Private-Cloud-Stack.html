Arsenal: Private Cloud Stack

Nextcloud + LibreOffice + Mailcow. A complete replacement for the M365 and Google ecosystems.

Reclaim Your Productivity Suite

Reliance on Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace creates a dependency on proprietary formats and cloud infrastructure. This vendor lock-in is a strategic vulnerability. We deploy a fully open-source, self-hosted productivity suite that provides all the functionality of the major providers without sacrificing control of your data.

Core Components:

  • Nextcloud: A powerful, self-hosted platform for file sharing, collaboration, and communication. It serves as the central hub for your organization's data.
  • LibreOffice: A feature-rich office suite that is fully compatible with all Microsoft Office file formats. We deploy and manage LibreOffice Online, enabling real-time collaborative editing within Nextcloud.
  • Mailcow / Zimbra: A complete and secure email server solution. We build and manage your sovereign email infrastructure, freeing you from reliance on external providers.

A Unified, Sovereign Ecosystem

By integrating these powerful open-source projects, we create a seamless and secure environment for your team's daily operations. This private cloud stack runs on your hardware, ensuring that you maintain absolute ownership and control over your critical business information.
