Arsenal: Hardened Identity

OpenLDAP and Keycloak implementations to replace Active Directory and proprietary SSO providers.

Your Identity is Not for Sale

Identity is the cornerstone of security. Entrusting it to a proprietary system like Active Directory creates a single point of failure. We engineer resilient, open-source identity and access management (IAM) solutions that provide superior security and flexibility.

Core Components:

  • OpenLDAP: A robust and highly performant user directory that serves as the single source of truth for your identities.
  • Keycloak: A powerful open-source IAM solution for modern applications, providing Single Sign-On (SSO) and multi-factor authentication (MFA).
