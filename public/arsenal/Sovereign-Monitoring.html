Arsenal: Sovereign Monitoring

Full-stack observability using Uptime Kuma and Netdata. Know exactly what your hardware is doing.

If You Don't Monitor It, You Don't Own It

You cannot secure what you cannot see. Proprietary monitoring solutions often send your sensitive performance and operational data to third-party clouds, creating another vector for data leakage. We deploy a powerful, self-hosted monitoring stack that gives you real-time, high-resolution insights into your entire infrastructure, from bare metal to application code.

Core Components:

  • Netdata: Deployed on every server and workstation, Netdata provides unparalleled real-time performance monitoring with thousands of metrics, collected per second. Its dashboards provide immediate, granular insight into system health.
  • Uptime Kuma: A user-friendly, self-hosted monitoring tool that provides comprehensive uptime monitoring for all your internal and external services. It acts as the central dashboard for service availability.
  • Prometheus + Grafana (Optional): For enterprises requiring long-term metric storage and advanced analytics, we deploy and manage a Prometheus and Grafana stack, the industry standard for open-source metrics and visualization.

Total Operational Awareness

Our monitoring solutions provide you with complete visibility into your infrastructure's health and performance. This proactive approach allows us to identify and resolve issues before they impact your operations, ensuring the stability and reliability of your sovereign systems.
