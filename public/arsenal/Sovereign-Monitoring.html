You cannot secure what you cannot see. We deploy a powerful, self-hosted monitoring stack that gives you real-time, high-resolution insights into your entire infrastructure, from bare metal to application code, without sending data to third-party clouds.

Core Components:

  • Netdata: Deployed on every server, providing thousands of real-time performance metrics per second.
  • Uptime Kuma: A user-friendly, self-hosted tool that provides a central dashboard for service uptime and availability.
