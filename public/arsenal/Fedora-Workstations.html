Arsenal: Fedora Workstations

Cutting-edge productivity for developers and creators. No licenses, no telemetry, just raw performance.

The Sovereign Desktop

The desktop environment is the primary interface for your organization's talent. It should be a tool that empowers, not a system that extracts. Fedora Workstation is the premier open-source desktop operating system, providing a secure, stable, and powerful platform for the most demanding tasks. By migrating your teams to Fedora, you reclaim control from proprietary vendors like Microsoft and Apple.

Key Advantages:

  • No Licensing Fees: Eliminate the significant operational expense of Windows or macOS licenses. Fedora is free, forever.
  • Zero Telemetry: Unlike Windows, Fedora does not collect or transmit user activity data. Your work remains your own.
  • Up-to-Date Software: Fedora offers a balance of cutting-edge software with a stable core, providing access to the latest development tools and libraries.
  • Enhanced Security: With a foundation in Red Hat Enterprise Linux and features like SELinux enabled by default, Fedora is one of the most secure desktop environments available.
  • Customizable and Flexible: We tailor Fedora deployments to your exact needs, with pre-configured software, security policies, and user environments.

Our Migration Process

We provide a seamless, zero-downtime migration path from Windows to Fedora. Our process includes data transfer, application compatibility analysis, and user training to ensure a smooth transition and immediate productivity gains.
