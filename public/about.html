The Archon Method

Traditional IT support is broken. We are implementing a revolutionary process we call the Archon Method. This unique system combines the best of human expertise with specialized AI. Our human consultants—the Deed Holders—are augmented by a fleet of custom-built AI "ArchonAgents," each an expert in a specific domain (e.g., Linux kernel panics, database optimization).

How It Works:

  1. Initial Triage: An AI orchestrator analyzes the issue to identify the core problem domain.
  2. Agent Deployment: The relevant ArchonAgent is deployed to perform deep diagnostics and propose solutions.
  3. Human Verification: The Deed Holder reviews the AI's findings and oversees implementation.

This hybrid approach doesn't just solve problems faster; it creates a living, learning system that becomes more effective with every issue resolved.
