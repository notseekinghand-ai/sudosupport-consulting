The Process to Become a Deed Holder

Your identity and reputation within our system are secured by a unique $DEED NFT on the Base L2 network. To begin the process, you must provide a wallet address to receive your Deed.

1. Acquire ETH on Base

Our network operates on Base, a secure, low-cost Ethereum Layer 2. You will need Ether (ETH) on Base for transaction fees (gas). You can acquire this by:

  • Bridging: If you already have ETH on Ethereum, you can move it to Base using the official Base Bridge.
  • On-ramps: You can purchase ETH directly onto the Base network through services like Coinbase or other crypto exchanges.

2. Get a Compatible Wallet

A self-custody crypto wallet is essential. If you are new to crypto, here are some user-friendly and secure wallets:

  • MetaMask: The most popular browser-based wallet.
  • Coinbase Wallet: A beginner-friendly wallet with excellent mobile and browser support.
  • Frame: A privacy-focused desktop wallet for Ethereum and its L2s.

Once you have a wallet, simply copy your public wallet address (it typically starts with "0x") and paste it into the field below.
