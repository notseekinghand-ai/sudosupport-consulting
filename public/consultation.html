Our Sovereign Economy

To ensure transparency and align our incentives with our clients, SudoSupport operates on a decentralized foundation built on the Base L2 blockchain. This is not just technology for technology's sake; it is a core part of our commitment to a world without intermediaries.

Why Blockchain?

  • Transparency: All funding and project expenditures can be publicly audited, ensuring funds are used to improve the system.
  • Decentralization: By using a public blockchain, we escape the fees and control of traditional banking and payment processors.
  • Ownership: The use of $DEED NFTs provides cryptographic proof of your relationship and stake within our ecosystem.

Getting Started: Your Wallet is Your Key

The consultation process requires a self-custody crypto wallet. The system does not create a wallet for you; you must provide your own. This ensures that you, and only you, have control over your digital identity and assets.

1. Acquiring ETH on Base:

Our network operates on Base, a secure and low-cost Ethereum Layer 2. To interact with it, you will need Ether (ETH) on the Base network for transaction fees (gas). You can acquire this by:

  • Bridging: If you already have ETH on Ethereum, you can move it to Base using the official Base Bridge.
  • On-ramps: You can purchase ETH directly onto the Base network through services like Coinbase.

2. Recommended Wallets:

If you are new to crypto, here are some user-friendly and secure wallets to get you started:

  • MetaMask: The most popular browser-based wallet, compatible with nearly every dApp.
  • Coinbase Wallet: A very beginner-friendly wallet with excellent mobile and browser support.
  • Frame: A privacy-focused desktop wallet for Ethereum and its L2s.
